How, exactly, does the widget work?

  1. The 300x250px widget is embedded in your website through a simple html code.
  2. Your readers submit links through the input box.
  3. Links are ordered based on relevant user interaction analytics.

How are submissions filtered and aggregated?

By the people! Content is sorted through a combination of analytics and timeliness. Our live algorithm is coming soon!

Can anyone really submit for free?

Yes! Everyone is welcome to submit a link for free. You can take two routes - submit for free, or pay for a prominent position within the widget.

Why an auction system (instead of CPM, CPC, or CPA -based pricing)?

This system, combined with in-depth analytics, will most accurately reflect the value of a position. Instead of artificially designating a price for a spot, whether for a time period or interaction rate, advertisers choose their expenditure based on current analytics information.

How is revenue split?

50/50! LigerTail disburses payment through check. Please contact us with special instructions.

How does the payment/auction system work?

When you decide to purchase a spot, you are brought to the payment page. There you can see analytics information, starting with page views, clicks-throughs, uniques, and closes (more coming soon). These analytics will help assess how much placement of your link is worth to you. Once you decide, you can pay with a major credit card.

When I pay for a spot, what guarantees do I have that I will stay on top?

None. Though this may sound scary, it benefits everyone. Before submitting, you must consider the quality and relevance of the linked content to the space that you are posting in. Unappealing, irrelevant ads will remain out of sight. High caliber, relevant content will stay on top, receiving higher click-through rates.

What analytics are provided?

Currently, we provide page views, clicks-throughs, uniques, and closes. We are constantly adding more analytics tools for you to make the best decision possible. (Link to analytics sample)

What widget sizes are there?

Right now we are offering a 300x250px widget, the standard iAB display ad unit, for super easy website integration. Contact us for information about other options.
